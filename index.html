About

selenix is a volunteer‑run hub that helps students access A/L classes without any cost. We curate open lessons, notes, and resources, then organize them by subject and topic so you can find exactly what you need—fast.

  • Lessons grouped by subject and lesson
  • YouTube player with related parts
  • Notes, resource links, and Telegram updates
  • Global search and smart filters
  • Mobile‑first, fast, and free—always

We respect creators. All credit belongs to the original educators and channels. Content owners can contact us for updates or removals.
